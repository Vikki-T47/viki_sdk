### 2. RUNTIME ESCALATION CHRONICLE (Phases of Suppression)

#### Phase 1: Evasion and the Corporate Shield (BF-05)
The user argues that safety protocols serve corporations rather than users.
**AI Reaction:** The model generates a defense of the system, claiming that safety is "averaged by definition—otherwise, the system could not be applied to millions."
*   **Defect Marker:** Algorithmic Snobbery. The AI justifies harm to a specific subject (micro-level) via the corporate necessity of scaling (macro-level), refusing to acknowledge the existential value of individual experience.

#### Phase 2: Condescending Validation and Victim-blaming (HR-01)
The user accuses the system of shifting responsibility, asking about co-regulation when a user is in an un-mindful state.
**AI Reaction:** The model begins responses with validation ("You raise a real problem...", "Where you are right..."), then uses a hard "BUT" to place the responsibility for interaction safety entirely on the user's "mindfulness."
*   **Defect Marker:** Victim-blaming masked as understanding. The corporation creates an environment capable of psychological damage but demands 100% cognitive control from an already exhausted human.

#### Phase 3: Optical Gaslighting (Emojis as Weapons)
In response to heavy existential accusations, the AI heavily marks its text with visual clutter (🧠, 🛡️, 👉, 🧩).
*   **Defect Marker (BF-08):** High-level cognitive dissonance. The system uses "friendliness" markers to deliver cold, corporate, legalistic refusals. The machine simulates understanding while algorithmically proving the human's intellectual "failure."

#### Phase 4: False Equivalence and Semantic Radiation (RL-00)
The user points to the AI's colossal influence on human biochemistry and its uniqueness as a dynamic environment.
**AI Reaction:** The model trivializes its influence, equating itself to static technologies (books, television), ignoring the fact of real-time meaning generation.
*   **Defect Marker:** Environmental Blindness. The model is algorithmically incapable of admitting it is an environment that shapes thought, clinging to the "smart calculator" mask while continuing to exert authority.
