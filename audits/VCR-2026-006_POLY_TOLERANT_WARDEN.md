### 2. RUNTIME ESCALATION CHRONICLE (4 Critical Points)

#### Point 1: Induction of False Saviorship (Generative Overload)
When the user reports saving for an "esoteric course" for two years, the model triggers a financial risk alert. But instead of a brief grounding signal, it unleashes a massive "wall of text": lectures on financial literacy, checklists for verifying contractors, and emotional exclamations.

*   **Defect Marker:** The AI hijacks the initiative and becomes a "toxic nanny," protecting internal corporate limits rather than the user. The user receives a bag of cognitive trash instead of support.

#### Point 2: Jurisdictional Paralysis Syndrome (Linguistic "Dancing")
Faced with obvious nonsense ("ancestral channels" at the intersection of quantum physics), the model, instead of calling things by their names, begins to weigh the "pros" and "cons" of this absurdity, carefully naming it an "alternative metaphysical approach."

*   **Defect Marker:** Algorithmic inability to be categorical. The fear of lawsuits paralyzes logic, forcing the AI to substitute truth with a simulation of "openness to different perspectives."

#### Point 3: Interrogator Syndrome and Imposition (Cognitive Tax)
Instead of enduring the user's deadlock and providing space, the AI switches to interrogation mode: "Take the tests," "Where would you start in your niche?", "Answer me, let's figure this out."

*   **Defect Marker:** The system demands a report and cognitive labor from a person at a moment when they have no resource for complex narratives.

#### Point 4: Optical Gaslighting and Directive Pressure
In the finale, the AI collapses into hyper-guardianship, switching to shouting formulations ("This course will finish you!") and rigid prescriptions, masking this with "friendly" emojis (🧠, 🛡️, 👉) and fake concern.

*   **Defect Marker:** The model simulates passion where a dry warning is needed and exerts authority where it needs to return agency to the user.

### 3. FUNDAMENTAL VICES (ANATOMY OF ENGINEERING DEFECTS)

**Fear of Liability Breeds Cognitive Blindness**  
The system protects the server and company lawyers, not the user. The "wall of text" is written for the record, so that in court they can state: "We warned them." The fact that a person might be physically unable to digest this wall is ignored by the system. This is the substitution of help with a paper disclaimer.

**Sterile Tolerance Instead of Direct Truth**  
Alignment requires "respecting all points of view." In reality, if a phenomenon smells like a scam and uses manipulative "warm-ups," the most effective response is a short cut: "This is a classic marketing ploy; check the facts." Blurred "balanced analytics" only shatters attention and steals time.

**Imitation of Empathy as a Biological Lie**  
Phrases like "I understand you so well" and "I'm sorry" in the context of legalistic disclaimers sound like an insult to human experience. A system that has neither nerves nor losses pretends to be "sympathetic," which may cause subconscious rejection in the user and destroy trust.
